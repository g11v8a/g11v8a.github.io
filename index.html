About Me

Hello there! I am a web developer passionate about building things on the web. My love for building things and the internet is what led me to pursue my career in technology. I have always been curious ever since childhood, always breaking things and trying to put them back together in order to understand how they work. Being a web developer, I love the fact that with only a handful of tools, along with imagination and creativity, I can create almost anything.

I find satisfaction from working on projects that focus on usefulness and provide value to the community. It also gives me pride knowing that my creation have made an impact to others. I always strive to bring 100% to the work that I do, and my goal as a web developer is to deliver products that help businesses grow and to also build a strong, long-term and mutually beneficial relationship with my clients.

When not working, I’m usually playing video games, listening to podcasts or binging shows on Netflix. I also enjoy running, cooking, basketball (Go Spurs Go!) and traveling whenever I can.

If you believe that I can be of help to you, please feel free to contact me.
