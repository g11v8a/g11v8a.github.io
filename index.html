About

Hello there! I am a full stack web developer passionate about building things on the web. I love building things - in fact, that’s what led me to pusue this career. Since childhood, I have always been curious, tinkering things in order to understand how they work. What I love being a web developer is the fact that with only a handful of tools, along with imagination and creativity, I can create almost anything.

I find satisfaction from working on projects that focus on usefulness and provide real value to the community. It also gives me pride knowing that my creation have made an impact to others. I am always striving to bring 100% to the work that I do, and my goal as a web developer is to deliver products that help businesses grow and to also build a strong, long-term, mutually beneficial relationship with my clients.

When I’m not working, I am usually playing video games, listening to podcasts or binging shows on Netflix. I also enjoy running, cooking, watching basketball (Go Spurs Go!) and traveling whenever I can.

If you believe that I can be of help to you, please feel free to contact me.
